Dream Hunters DAO

Product Vision

The vision of Dream Hunters is to build a community that empowers people to share their dreams, connect with other dreamers around the world, and fund their journeys through decentralised crowdfunding.

My Role

My role in Dream Hunters is to understand the vision the product and lead the charge with making it come to life! I lead a team of five developers and three designers from all over the globe and host weekly stand ups to ensure the team stays on track.
